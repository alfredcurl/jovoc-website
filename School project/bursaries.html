Academic Bursaries

The school offers a limited number of bursaries to some of the best-performing students in each class. The bursaries are academically based and are funded by the school, sister schools through exchange programs, old boys, school well-wishers, NGOs, catholic funding programs, etc. The school may also offer half bursaries to top-performing students who are unable to raise the entire school fees and are highly disciplined.
